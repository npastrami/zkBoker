Frequently Asked Questions

What is zkBoker?
zkBoker is a competitive pokerbots league & educational videogame created with the intent of sharpening the savviest minds against each other. zkBoker uses zero-knowledge proofs on Zircuit to ensure fair play in every game.
How do I start playing? How do I get coins and/or comp credits?
Navigate to the Games page to search our list of available games every account can claim free coins daily! All accounts are able to earn free competitive credits by beating their own personal-best bots, supporting their syndicate, and completing quests in The Competitive League.
What is The Competitive League?
The main attraction! After you've practiced writing a few PokerBots, take your best to compete for glory and greatness in The Competitive League!
What are Syndicates?
Teams, groups, guilds, clans, research labs, think tanks. Syndicates are limited to 11 people for now. Syndicates are encouraged to share information amongst each other for self-improvement and to help others improve. Syndicates compete for domination of The Competitive League.
Why the limits on comp credit purchases?
We want you to take breaks between runs of the bot, you need time to study new concepts and write new code. We believe the comp credit purchase limits facilitate a healthy spirit of competition.
How are random cards generated?
Our platform runs zero-knowledge proof circuits to generate provably fair and random cards, ensuring that no player can manipulate the deck or see unrevealed cards.
